Air Robotics, LLC, an aerospace company, designs and manufactures wing body airborne vehicle systems. Its airborne vehicle system enables customers to swap payloads to perform multiple missions using a single airframe. The companys airborne vehicle systems are used in remote sensing, ISR, cyber/EW, SIGINT, lethal/non-lethal delivery, mapping, and airborne scientific applications. Air Robotics, LLC was incorporated in 2011 and is based in Charleston, West Virginia.
