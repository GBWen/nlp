Health Fidelity offers solutions for healthcare organizations participating in risk-based payment programs that enable them to identify, assess and quantify the risk of their patient populations. Our solution streamlines an otherwise manual risk adjustment process with our cutting-edge technology that combines natural language processing (NLP) and big data analytics to automatically extract valuable insights from medical charts. Equipped with this proprietary technology and a team of industry experts, Health Fidelity can help organizations optimize their risk adjustment process to increase efficiency, achieve better compliance, and maximize value through improved identification of risk factors. Our solutions currently serve three markets  Medicare Advantage, Qualified Health Plans (QHP) under the Affordable Care Act (ACA), and Medicare Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs).
