CALM Energy was spun out of Columbia Universitys Center for Computational Learning Systems to deploy machine learning based technology in buildings and utility grids. It was founded by [Albert Boulanger](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/albert-boulanger), [Roger Anderson](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/roger-anderson), [Hubert Delany](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/hubert-delany), [Fred Seibel](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/fred-seibel), and [John Johnson](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/john-johnson-5). The firm is based in New York, N.Y."
