"CrowdIndex allows analysis of the real-time sentiment and volume relating to particular markets or market-related topics. It uses advanced machine learning tools to mine social data in a way that is relevant to the language of financial markets. So its like a Radian6 for traders. And it enables the user to track shifts in sentiment and volume for a particular market and amongst different groups of  people - sentiment ""crowds"" - based on filters such as social influence, age, geography, even keywords in users bios.
