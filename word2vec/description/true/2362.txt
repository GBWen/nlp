HealthRhythms makes it easy to measure and care for everyones mental health. By seamlessly gathering data about everyday behaviors and routines (our behavioral rhythms), HealthRhythms provides a more comprehensive and clinically grounded understanding of mental wellbeing. Our solution leverages deep machine learning and predictive analytics to create novel health assessments and just-in-time personalized interventions that improve the care delivery experience for patients, health plans, payers and research organizations. HealthRhythms was founded by a unique team of mental health care pioneers and information scientists at the forefront of health sensing.
