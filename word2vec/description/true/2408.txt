Courses: We have developed over 60 one-week STEM courses in partnership with 20 of the worlds leading STEM organizations including MIT, Harvard and Stanford Universities, the Museum of Science in Boston, the Center for Mathematical Talent, and Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution. Each of our courses is designed to have students work to find solutions to real-world problems, including a number of the National Academy of Engineerings Grand Challenges for Engineering.  Courses are categorized under six topics not traditionally seen in middle school education: Challenges of Engineering; Genetics, DNA & the Human Body; The Science of Smart Cities; Our Transforming World; Time, Space and Other Dimensions; and Robotics and Electronics.
