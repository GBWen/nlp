Primary function of the ICENT is lowering the risk of developing new and innovative products by using ICENTs expert staff, state-of-the-art equipment and infrastructure to bridge the gap between research institutions and the industry. The risk is lowered by taking over the development of - and -prototypes and early-stage manufacturing processes using government or industrial funding to: (i) release products to start-up companies financed using venture capital or (ii) further government funding all with the goal of new technologies commercialization.
