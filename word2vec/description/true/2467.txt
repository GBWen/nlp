The companys flagship solution  IntuScan  is a semantic text analytics platform that combines advanced ontology-based technology with NLP algorithms to provide users with automated understanding of unstructured text in a variety of languages. Spanning the entire range of tasks to extract meaning from text  from language (and dialect) identification, through entities detection to discovering sales opportunities in social media, IntuScan provides topic identification and categorization, and in-depth understanding of the inner meaning of text that would otherwise go unnoticed."
