Olio City is your go-to app for authentic city discovery, personalized to your specific taste and preferences. How do we do it By marrying original content with an algorithm that learns user preferences. Our team of cultural aficionados curate a database of the best local experiences a city has to offer from events, restaurants, attractions and more. The app then tailors results to each users interests, based on their likes and dislikes. Whether its jazz music or Japanese fusion, these preferences travel with you  across town and across state lines to offer recommendations that fit your taste.
