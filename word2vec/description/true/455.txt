WobbleWorks is an emerging toy and robotics company with big ideas. Their mission is to create affordable, cutting edge toys and robots that inspire and delight our customers the world over.  Founded in 2011, WobbleWorks is led by Peter Dilworth and Maxwell Bogue, experienced toy makers and enthusiasts. WobbleWorks products include Wigglerz (Spring 13), and large walking dinosaurs such as Butch, the life-size robotic protoceratops, built for museums and theme parks."
