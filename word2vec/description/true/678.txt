Genetics Squared, Inc. provides solutions for personalized medicines in Michigan. The company focuses on offering life sciences solutions. Its Evolver technology, a machine learning technique that produces mathematical models that describe a dataset based on what specific questions are being asked about that dataset. The companys technology is applied in various areas of clinical and preclinical biomedical research, such as for clinical oncology, infectious disease, nephrology, and autoimmune disease. Genetics Squared, Inc. was founded in 2001 and is based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.
