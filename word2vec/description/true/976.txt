"KangarooHealth is a digital health start-up company enabling hospitals and large orthopedic groups to reduce readmission and cut post-surgery care costs by over 55% for joint replacement surgeries.  KangarooHealth develops a patient-risk big data analytics and intervention platform-as-a-service powered by proprietary machine learning and computer vision algorithms. KangarooHealth solution intelligently predicts each patients risk of readmission based on patients post-surgery rehabilitation performance and compliance. KangarooHealth develops intelligent computer-vision based rehabilitation systems as the innovative means to track patients post-surgery clinical performance and compliance in patients homes. Patient receive real-time personalized rehabilitation care feedback via engaging motion-controlled rehabilitation games at home, saving them time and over $1200 from visits to outpatient physical therapy clinics.
